This is Sonic

Sonic the Hedgehog is basically a blue blur of chaos, speed, and questionable physics. He runs faster than logic, defies gravity like it’s a mere suggestion, and eats chili dogs like they fuel his cosmic existence. Created by Sega in 1991, he was supposed to be a cool rival to Mario, but instead, he became a pop culture phenomenon with wildly inconsistent games, insane fandom theories, and enough spin-offs to make your head spin (literally, like a spin dash).

His best friend is a two-tailed fox who flies like a helicopter, his rival is a red echidna who gets tricked *every* time, and his enemy is a round dude with a mustache and an infinite supply of evil robots. The Sonic franchise has bangers like *Sonic Adventure 2* and disasters like *Sonic '06*, but it keeps running (pun intended).

Also, let's not forget the insane Sonic lore—multiverses, time travel, alien gods, talking swords?! Somehow, this blue rodent has survived everything: bad games, weird cartoons, terrifying movie redesigns, and the darkest corners of DeviantArt. Love him or hate him, Sonic is eternal. Gotta go fast… but where? Nobody really knows.
